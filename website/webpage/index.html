Overview

Integrated Plant Disease Detection System

In the realm of precision agriculture, the detection of plant diseases is crucial for maintaining crop health and optimizing yields. Our project leverages advanced technologies, specifically the Segment Anything Model (SAM) and YOLOv11, to develop a robust model for detecting diseased plants based on their leaves and stems.

Leaf, Stem and Fruit Analysis

The model first identifies the plant using its leaves, stems, and fruits. It classifies the plant as healthy or diseased.

Our model follows a straightforward three-step process:

  1. Image Input & Segmentation: Takes a plant image and uses SAM to precisely cut out individual leaf/stem regions
  2. Disease Analysis: YOLO examines these segments to identify specific diseases
  3. Report Generation: Creates a detailed report containing:
    • Type of disease detected.
    • How confident the model is about its diagnosis(confidence score)?
    • What percentage of the plant is affected?
